 

The ServiceNow Certified System Administrator (CSA) exam confirms you have the knowledge and skills to configure, implement, and maintain the ServiceNow system. This certification validates their ability to navigate the platform, configure applications, manage databases, and implement self-service and automation. Certified individuals handle data migration, integrations, and customizations to support efficient and scalable ServiceNow operations.

Passing this proctored exam awards you a CSA certification and a Credly digital badge to highlight your skills and achievements online.

Intended Audience:

ServiceNow customers, partners, employees, and others seeking administrator-level certification.
Recommended Experience (see the exam blueprint for more):

Three to six months of hands-on experience using and maintaining a ServiceNow instance.
Exam Preparation:

Review the System Administrator Credential Path for the list of recommended courses. Course completion is not required but highly recommended.
We strongly recommend completing the training before beginning the exam registration and scheduling processes. These courses are designed to align with the exam blueprint and significantly improve exam readiness.
Review the CSA Exam Blueprint for exam objectives and other important details.
Gain real-world experience, create, test, and learn on your own ServiceNow environment.
